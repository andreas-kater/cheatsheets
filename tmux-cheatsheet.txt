Ctrl+s      enter tmux mode
  %         split screen horizontally
  "         split screen vertically
Ctrl+h      switch to window to the left
Ctrl+j      switch to window on top
Ctrl+k      switch to window below
Ctrl+l      switch to window to the right

Ctrl+n      open new session
Ctrl+s, s   list open sessions
Ctrl+s, j   rename current session
Ctrl+s, K   kill current session
